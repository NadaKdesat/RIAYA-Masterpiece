Home care services departments

We provide health solutions to improve people's lives by offering an elite group of doctors and specialists to diagnose and treat patients in their homes.

Home Visit

Receive medical care at home from experienced specialists, including nurses, physical therapists, and occupational therapists. Services may include treatments, rehabilitation support, or assistance with mobility.

Remote Consultation

Connect with specialized doctors via video calls or text messages for quick consultations. Get initial diagnoses, treatment advice, or referrals to specialists, all from the comfort of your home.

Emergency

Instantly request emergency medical care or ambulance services. Your location is automatically detected via GPS to ensure a quick response during critical situations.
